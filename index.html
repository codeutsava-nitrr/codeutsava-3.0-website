1.​Blockchain For Fighting insurance fraud 
The blockchain is an incorruptible digital ledger of economic transactions that can be programmed to record not just financial transactions but virtually everything of value.

Blockchain is a distributed database that maintains information/order/records called blocks. The data grows constantly, so a ‘completed block’ is linked with a new block with fresh information. Each new set of block contains a timestamp and references and identifies the previous block by a hashing function, forming a chain.

Blockchains are secure by design as they are inherently resistant to modification- once recorded, the data in a block cannot be altered retroactively. The technology can be used to record transactions between two parties effectively and in a verifiable and permanent manner. Moreover, Blockchain itself can be programmed to automatically trigger transactions.
                                
Ethereum is an open software platform based on blockchain technology that enables developers to build and deploy decentralized applications. 
                                
Some of the major problems that can be effectively solved using this could be :
                                
1. BlockChain for fighting insurance fraud.
2. Blockchain for Supply Chain Management.
3. Blockchain for Know Your Customer (KYC).
4. Blockchain for Smart Wallets.
5. Blockchain for Identity Management.
                                
The teams are encouraged to provide innovative solutions to any of the problems above (or any other domain) which can benefit greatly from the Blockchain technology.
                                
Note: The teams should build a blockchain based system and it must use Ethereum platform.

2.​Smart Locks
One of the major problems faced during transportation of goods is that they get tampered with or sometimes unauthorized users get access to them leading to misuse and loss to the company.

Incidents of theft from delivery vehicles are occurring nationwide and seem to be increasing. These incidents are causing havoc across the country’s supply chains leading to loss of hundreds of crores.

In such case a Smart Lock (Digital Safe) that protects valuable assets in transit and provides access to only authorized users could be useful. These locks should also be able to detect tampering and attempts to unlock by unauthorized persons and at unauthorized locations.
The admin of the system should be able to add authorized users who can have access  these locks. All the data should be stored on a Cloud for future reference and ease of access by other concerned authorities whenever required.

The teams are encouraged to innovate further to design and implement an IoT- based system to ensure safety of goods.

Artificial Intelligence professionals say that they can. Scientists predict that machines will perform tasks such as translation, image or speech recognition, and decision making.

Machine learning is an application of AI that discovers patterns in data and predicts outcomes without being explicitly programmed. Machine learning uses data to create, test, and train AI.

4.Android
Jivandeep Health Services is a startup which focuses on providing health services through social media impact. Ravindra Singh Kshatri says, “We want to build a connected health service system to facilitate  in organ donation, blood donation and so much more”.

In case of an emergency, the most important life-saver is Blood. Blood Banks are the main providers of blood, which receive blood from various donors, monitor the blood group database for emergencies and makes the information available to the hospital whenever needed. The major problem faced by the blood bank is the availability of the donor at the right time.

We, hereby require a system where people can help each other in need. We need a Blood Donation Management application (Android) where:

• The Blood banks can timely update the Blood Stock availability.

• Donor can register themselves to donate.

• User can find blood availability nearby him/her.

• The ‘urgency time’ of a blood requirement

• User can access information about blood banks, hospitals or donors as required (by matching a particular or related field) and reach out to them through the App.

• Application tends to provide list of blood banks in user area.

• A large number of blood donors are attracted by the  application.

• Since almost everyone carries a mobile phone with him/her, it ensures instant location tracking and communication.

• Registered user, who can donate blood, can pledge to donate and will be able to access the service.

• In this application, we are using the GPS technology that will be used to guide the way to the blood bank.

• The user will get the route to reach the desired location and he/she won't have to ask manually, in order to save time.

5.Augmented reality​ (​AR​)
Augmented reality​ (​AR​) is an interactive experience of a real-world environment where the objects that reside in the real-world are "augmented" by computer-generated perceptual information, sometimes across multiple sensory modalities, including visual, auditory, haptic, somatosensory, and olfactory.The primary value of augmented reality is that it brings components of the digital world into a person's perception of the real world, and does so not as a simple display of data, but through the integration of immersive sensations that are perceived as natural parts of an environment. 

Virtual reality​ (​VR​) is an interactive computer-generated experience taking place within a simulated environment. It incorporates mainly auditory and visual feedback, but may also allow other types of sensory feedback.

Mixing the real world with computer-generated images could change forever the way we interact with our surroundings. From driving to video games to healthcare, Augmented reality and Virtual reality could blend our perceptions and senses.

Your challenge is to use this buzzing tech to build cool hacks, some inspirations to take are storytelling through AR/VR or building new forms of interfaces. This technology allows you to widen your horizon and build innovative hacks for uplifting the living of fellow beings in general.
